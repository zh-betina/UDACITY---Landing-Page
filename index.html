About

Our team and our work

...they are for us a big part of our life. Who are we? We are simple women: somebody's daughters, mothers, wives, girlfriends, neighbours. Businesswomen, artists, ingenieurs.

What we have in common is a strong belief, that the art of make-up isn't vain, isn't there to cover up your imperfections

It's there to bring joy, colors and express our personalities

We help you...

to learn how to put together colors and various techniques for best make-up result taking into account your personality, your preferences, lifestyle.

Offer

Group workshops, individual makeover/metamorphosis meetings and courses

We work with groups (mostly companies) as well as individual clients interested in self-development, enhancement of personal features.

Time, place, exact planning and pricing are adjusted in according to the specific needs of our clients
